Beats is a Flock App enabling users to create a music playlist on the go. User can make use of slashcommand to enter the names of their desired playlist songs and beats(bot) would create a youtube playlist for the user and add all the songs in it(and provide the link).The playlist could be played until midnight ,after that the playlist gets automatically deleted.Users can also create predefined playlists like top 20 songs of bollywood, international using the same command. 
The moto behind this app was to give a platform for sharing of playlist among friends and also to make playlist quickly and save time required for searching and playing music.Users can create their playlist in the start of their work itself  and then concentrate on the work instead of selecting music entire day.
Future work will include sharing of playlist among teammates ,automatic generation of playlist depending on users mood[to be taken from user]
